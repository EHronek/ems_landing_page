Our Story

After spending years in HR departments drowning in paperwork and outdated systems, we knew there had to be a better way. EmpowerHR was born during my time at ALX as a portfolio project, but it quickly evolved into a passion. The 3-month development journey taught me how to balance user needs with technical constraints, resulting in this streamlined solution that real HR teams actually want to use.

This project combines my experience in human resources with cutting-edge technology to solve real pain points I've witnessed firsthand in the workplace.
